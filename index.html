Waste Guide

Know your waste, save our planet

Proper segregation is the first step to a cleaner city. EarthAlly makes it simple.

📄

Dry Waste

Paper, cardboard, metals, glass, and non-wet plastics. Clean and separate before disposal.

Blue Bin
🥬

Wet Waste

Food scraps, vegetable peels, garden waste, and other organic biodegradable material.

Green Bin
🧴

Plastic Waste

Bottles, bags, containers, wrappers. Rinse before recycling to avoid contamination.

Orange Bin
💻

E-Waste

Old phones, cables, batteries, bulbs. Never mix with regular bins — drop at certified centres.
